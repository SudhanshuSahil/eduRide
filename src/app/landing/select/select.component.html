Students have to submit these details to request a ride-

  1. Name and contact details
  2. Home and centre pincodes
  3. Admit card details

Alumni/Volunteers can help in three ways-

  1. Drive the students themselves
  2. Arrange for students to travel
  3. Contribute money to IITDAA to facilitate cabs
